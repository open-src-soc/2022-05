Registration

Note to sponsors and booths renters: please wait for the mail with your personal code for the free registration.

Registration is now open here, and is done through the Linux Foundation.

Participation to the Spring 2022 RISC-V Week is either on site, with maximum opportunities to chat and network with speakers, exhibitors and other attendees during the three days, or remote, where the networking experience will be more than limited.

Furthermore, a social event with dinner will take place in the form of a cruise on the Seine, starting and returning not far from the conference venue – more details to follow. Please note that there is a limited number of seats on the boat, and that they are allocated on a first come, first served basis.

The registration fees to the Spring 2022 RISC-V Week are the following:

Registration Fee
On site (three days pass) 340.00 € ex. VAT
Social event on Wednesday 4th evening (1) 70.00 € ex. VAT
On-line (three days, only the sessions) 85.00 € ex. VAT
(1) An on site registration is required to participate to the social event.
