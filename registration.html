Registration

Participation to the Spring 2022 RISC-V Week could be either on site, with maximum opportunities to chat and network with speakers, exhibitors and other attendees during the three days, or remote, where the networking experience will be more than limited.

Furtehrmore, a social event with dinner will be organized on Wednesday 5th evening.

The registration fees to the Spring 2022 RISC-V Week are the following:

Registration Fee
On site (three days pass) 340 € ex. VAT
Social event on Wednesday 5th evening (1) 50 € ex. VAT
On-line 50 €
(1) An on site registration is required to participate to the social event.
