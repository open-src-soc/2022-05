Exhibition

The Spring 2022 RISC-V Week will be held in Paris on May 3-5 2022. This three-days event is a unique opportunity to present your tools, IPs, products, services, and collaborative projects to a high value audience of engineers, developers, executives and researchers who are key members to the world’s most advanced RISC-V and open source hardware community.

For the first time, the Spring 2022 RISC-V Week will feature an exhibition with a number of booths available for rent.

Renting a booth

A booth will consist of:

  • A two-seats table.
  • Two chairs.
  • A poster stand.
  • Two accounts on the WiFi network.
  • Two electrical outlets.
  • Free registration for one person for the three-days event.
  • Free registration for one person to the social event.

In addition, exhibitors will be able to make a short presentation (max. 5 min., approx. 3 slides) of their company and their booth to the audience, on Tuesday 3rd morning.

The time frame of the exhibition adheres to the conference's program as follows:

Sessions Tuesday 3rd Wednesday 4th Thursday 5th
Morning Closed (booths set-up) Open Open
Lunch Official Opening Open Open
Afternoon Open Open Closed (booths wind down)

The cost for a single booth is 1,450.00 € ex. VAT.

To rent a booth, or to get more information, please get in touch with us at contact@open-src-soc.org.
