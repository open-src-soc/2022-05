Exhibition

The Spring 2022 RISC-V Week, held in Paris on May 3-5 2022, is a unique opportunity to present your tools, IPs, products and services to a high value audience of engineers, developers, executives and researchers who are at the core of the design of the world’s most advanced RISC-V and open source hardware systems.

The Spring 2022 RISC-V Week will feature an exhibition whith a number of booths available for rent.

A booth will consist of:

  • A two-seats table.
  • Two chairs.
  • A poster stand.
  • Two accounts on the WiFi network.
  • Two electrical outlets.
  • Free registration for one person for the three-days event.
  • Free registration for one person to the social event.

The timeframe of the exhibition is the following:

  • Tuesday 3rd, morning. The exhibition has not yet open. Access is provided to set-up the booths.
  • Tuesday 3rd, lunch time. Official opening of the exhibition.
  • Tuesday 3rd, afternoon: The exhibition is open throughout the conference day.
  • Wednesday 4th, full day: The exhibition is open throughout the conference day.
  • Thursday 5th, morning: The exhibition open on the morning and lasts thoughout the lunch.
  • Thursday 5th, afternoon: The exhibition closes right after lunch. Access is provided to dismantle the booths.

The cost is 1,440.00 €/booth ex. VAT.

To rent a booth, or to get more information, please get in touch with our team at booths@open-src-soc.org.
