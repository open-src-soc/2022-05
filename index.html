“Spring 2022 RISC-V Week” on May 3-5 2022

The Spring 2022 RISC-V Week will be held in Paris Jussieu, and last three days from May 3rd to 5th.

After the disruptions of the 2020/2021 editions due to the pandemic, we have great hope for this year event to be held in real life! Although it will be possible to attend to the event online, we encourage participants to come to the event in Paris to resume the great networking that occured during the 2018 and 2019 editions!

This event will be open to students, academics and industrials.
