“RISC-V Week 2022” on May 3-5

The RISC-V Week 2022 will be held in Paris Jussieu in May 2022. It will last three days, from may the 3rd to may the 5th.

After the disruptions of the 2020/2021 event due to the pandemic, we have great hope for this year event to be held in real life! Although it will be possible to attend to the event online, we encourage participants to come to the event in Paris to restore the great networking that occured during the 2018 and 2019 editions!

This event will be open to students, academics and industrials.
