Welcome!

The Spring 2022 RISC-V Week will be held at the Auditorium of the Centre International de Conférences on the Jussieu Campus in Paris. It will take place May 3-5, 2022.

After the disruptions of the 2020/2021 editions due to the pandemic, we are happy to hold the Spring 2022 edition in real life and resume the great networking experience of the 2018 and 2019 editions! This year will also debut an industrial exhibition, a display of posters, and a social event on May 4 evening. Online attendence will also be possible, but limited to keynotes speeches and presentations.

The event will be open to students, academics and industrials.

Overview

The Spring 2022 RISC-V Week is co-organized by CEA, IRT-Nanoelec, and RISC-V International and gathers two events over three days:

Day Event Organizers
Tuesday-Wednesday, May 3-4 4th RISC-V Meeting CEA & IRT-Nanoelec
Thursday, May 5 RISC-V International Day RISC-V International

Those events will consist in a single track with keynotes, tutorials, panels and technical sessions.

An exhibition and a poster session will run in parallel to the speeches.

A social event will be organised on Wednesday May 4, evening.
